How does the 24/7 AI receptionist help compared to voicemail?
Unlike voicemail, the AI voice receptionist answers every call instantly, 24/7. It captures names, intent, and details, books appointments in real time, and texts back missed callers automatically. Businesses that replace voicemail with AI see higher lead capture and less revenue lost to missed calls.
Can the AI really book appointments on its own?
Yes. It integrates with your calendar or booking system, so the AI receptionist confirms appointments immediately — no human handoff required. That eliminates delays and boosts conversion.
What if multiple people call at once?
The AI handles multiple calls simultaneously without dropping them, using natural, brand-trained voice technology. Unlike human staff, it never gets overwhelmed during peak hours and works 24/7.
How does it capture leads from my website?
The AI chatbot greets every visitor instantly, engages with FAQs and your custom knowledge, and collects details like name, email, and questions. Leads are logged automatically into your CRM for follow-up and automation.
