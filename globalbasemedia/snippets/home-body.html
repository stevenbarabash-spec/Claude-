FAQ

Common questions

The questions we hear most from service-business owners — answered straight.

How much does a new website cost?
It depends on how many pages and features you need, so we scope it to your goals rather than force you into a fixed tier. Every project starts with a free design draft and a transparent quote — no surprises, no hidden fees. Call (561) 556-9190 or book a consult and we'll give you a real number for your business.
How long until my website is live?
You'll see your first custom design draft within 7 days of our kickoff call. Once you approve it, a typical site goes live shortly after — most businesses are up and running in a couple of weeks, depending on scope and how fast we get your content.
Will my website actually get me more customers?
That's the whole point. We build for conversion — fast load times, clear calls-to-action, click-to-call, and online booking — then back it with SEO and Google Ads so the right people find you. We optimize for booked jobs and revenue, not vanity metrics.
Do you handle SEO and Google Ads too, or just the website?
Both — they're our biggest sellers alongside web design. We build the site, rank it with SEO, and drive high-intent traffic with Google Ads, all connected to one CRM so every lead is tracked from click to customer. One partner instead of three vendors.
Will I show up on Google?
Yes. Every site is built on a technical SEO foundation (fast, mobile-first, structured data), and we optimize your Google Business Profile for local "near me" searches. For faster results, Google Ads puts you at the top of the page while your organic rankings build.
Do I own my website, domain, and accounts?
100%. You own your domain, your website, and your Google Ads and Analytics accounts — always. We never hold your assets hostage, and there's no long-term lock-in. If you ever leave, everything stays with you.
What if I already have a website?
We'll redesign and migrate it, keeping whatever's working and fixing what isn't — without losing your existing content or search rankings. We'll audit your current site for free and show you exactly where the opportunities are.
Do I have to write the content or supply photos?
No. We help with copywriting and can provide professional imagery. If you have your own photos and brand assets, even better — they make the site more personal — but nothing about the process waits on you.
Can I update the website myself later?
Yes. We build it so you can edit everyday things — text, images, hours, prices — yourself. For bigger changes or new pages, we're a quick call away at (561) 556-9190.
