FAQ

Virtual assistant questions, answered straight

How is this better than voicemail?
Unlike voicemail, the AI receptionist answers every call instantly, 24/7. It captures the caller's name, intent and details, books appointments in real time, and texts back missed calls automatically — recovering revenue that voicemail loses. Most callers who hit voicemail simply call the next business; the AI makes sure that never happens.
Can it really book appointments on its own?
Yes. The assistant is connected to your calendar. It offers real available time slots, books the appointment, sends the confirmation, and logs everything to your CRM — no human needed. You just see the appointment appear.
What if multiple people call at once?
The AI handles unlimited simultaneous conversations — five callers and twelve website chats at the same time are no problem. Nobody gets a busy signal, and nobody waits.
How does it capture leads from my website?
The chatbot greets visitors instantly, answers their questions, and collects their name and contact details in natural conversation. Every lead syncs straight to your CRM with the full conversation attached, and hot leads can trigger an instant text to your phone.
Does it work on social media too?
Yes — calls, SMS, website chat, WhatsApp, Facebook and Instagram messages all flow into one smart inbox. The AI steps in when you don't reply; if you jump in, it steps aside.
Will it sound robotic to my customers?
It's the most lifelike AI receptionist we've ever deployed — a voice and chat style so natural most customers never realize it's AI. We tune the tone to match your business, and you can ask us for a live demo any time to hear it yourself.
How fast can I get started?
Most businesses are live within 3–5 days. We start with a strategy session, custom-build the assistant around your services and calendar, then walk you through everything on an onboarding call — with a success manager fine-tuning speed, accuracy and tone as it runs.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Conversations and customer data live in your own CRM account — which you own — with industry-standard encryption. You have full access and full control, always.
