FAQ

Review system questions, answered straight

How does your Google review system work?
After every completed job, our system automatically texts and emails your customer a review request at the perfect moment — while the experience is fresh. Happy customers post straight to your Google profile; unhappy ones are routed to private feedback so you can fix the problem before it goes public. The AI even replies to every review for you.
Do I need to ask customers myself?
No. Asking in person can feel awkward or forced — an automated text with a simple review link makes it effortless, and the timing does the work. Businesses using automated review requests grow their Google reviews dramatically faster than those relying on asking by hand.
Can the system reply to reviews for me?
Yes. Our 24/7 AI responds to every Google review with a personalized, human-sounding message, so every customer feels heard. Replying consistently also signals an active business to Google, which supports your visibility.
Will it help me rank higher on Google?
Reviews are one of the strongest local ranking signals. A steady flow of fresh 5-star reviews — plus consistent replies — tells Google your business is active and trusted, which helps your position in the local map pack. Pair it with our SEO service and the two compound.
What happens if I get a bad review?
The system is built to catch problems early: unhappy customers are guided to private feedback before they post publicly, so you get the chance to make it right. If a bad review does land, we help you respond professionally — one calm, well-written reply often does more good than ten 5-stars.
How fast will I see results?
Most businesses see new reviews in the first week, with serious momentum inside 60 days. One dental client went from 145 reviews to 476 in three months and climbed to the top of local search results.
Which platforms do you cover?
Google is the priority because it drives local search — but the system also supports Facebook and other review platforms, and we can showcase your best reviews right on your website.
