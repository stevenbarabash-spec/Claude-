FAQ

SEO questions, answered straight

What does your SEO service include?
Everything local search rewards: technical SEO on your website, on-page optimization and content, your Google Business Profile, review growth, local citations, and link building — plus plain-English monthly reporting tied to calls and booked jobs, not just rankings.
How long does SEO take to work?
Expect early movement in 30–60 days and real momentum around 90 days, with results compounding from there. Timelines depend on your market, competition, and starting point — we set honest expectations in your free audit.
How much does SEO cost?
It depends on your market and how aggressive you want to be, so we scope a plan to your goals on a quick call. What's constant: month-to-month terms, you own everything, and reporting that shows exactly what your investment returns. Call (561) 556-9190 or book a consultation.
Can you guarantee #1 rankings?
No one can — and anyone who promises that is a red flag. What we can show is a consistent track record of climbing rankings for service businesses, and reporting honest enough that you can see it working (or hold us accountable).
What's the difference between SEO and Google Ads?
Ads buy immediate visibility at the top of the page; SEO earns lasting visibility that compounds and costs nothing per click. The strongest strategy is both: Ads for leads this week and keyword data, SEO for a moat that gets cheaper over time. We run both under one roof.
What is the local pack and how do I get in it?
The local pack is the map with three businesses that appears for searches like "dentist near me" — and it gets a huge share of clicks. Getting there takes an optimized Google Business Profile, steady reviews, consistent citations, and proximity signals. That trio is the heart of our local SEO work.
Do you write the content?
Yes. Service pages, location pages, and posts are written for you — in your voice, for your customers, optimized for the searches that matter. You review and approve everything.
What if I already have an SEO agency?
We'll audit what they've done — free. If it's good work, we'll tell you. If rankings have flatlined, you'll see exactly why, in plain English, and what we'd do differently. Switching is seamless: you own your site and profiles, so nothing is lost.
