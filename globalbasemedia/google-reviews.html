Frequently asked questions

How does your Google review system work?
Our system automatically sends review requests to your customers by text and email after a service is completed. Happy customers are guided to leave a 5-star review on Google; unhappy ones are routed to private feedback so issues are handled before they go public.
Do I need to ask customers myself?
No — the whole process is automated and hands-free. Every completed customer enters the review sequence at the perfect moment without you lifting a finger.
Can the system reply to reviews for me?
Yes. Our AI responds to every review 24/7 with personalized, human-sounding replies, keeping your profile active and professional.
Will it help me rank higher on Google?
Fresh, frequent 5-star reviews are a strong local ranking signal. As your review volume and rating climb, your Google Business Profile becomes more competitive in Maps and "near me" searches.
What if I get a bad review?
Our smart routing catches unhappy customers privately before they post, giving you a chance to make it right. If a public negative review does come in, our AI helps you respond quickly and professionally.
How fast will I see results?
