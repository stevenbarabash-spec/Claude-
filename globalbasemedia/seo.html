Getting started with SEO

Core SEO approaches for growth

Four pillars, one goal: get your business in front of the people already searching for what you do.

📝

On-Page SEO

Optimizing elements on your website so search engines read and rank your pages better — keyword placement in titles, headers, and content.

🔗

Off-Page SEO

Building authority beyond your site: backlinks from trusted sources, brand mentions, PR, guest blogging, influencer outreach, and social signals.

⚙️

Technical SEO

Making your site crawlable and fast — site speed and Core Web Vitals, secure HTTPS, fixing crawl errors, XML sitemaps, and structured data.

📍

Local SEO

Show up in Google Maps and "near me" searches — optimized Google Business Profile, review management, and citations in local directories.
