Risk Factors

Material Risks to Consider
Before Investing.

This is a private placement in an early-stage company. Understand the risks before making a commitment.

Early Stage Company
Green Evolution Technologies is pre-Series A with limited operating history. Past performance in trial phases does not guarantee future commercial results at scale.
Illiquid Investment
This is an illiquid private placement. There is no secondary market for these securities and you may not be able to sell your interest for an extended period or at all.
Manufacturing Scale Risk
The 30-facility production plan requires substantial capital, operational expertise, and execution. Delays or cost overruns could materially impact projected timelines and returns.
Regulatory Environment
Changes to USDA, EPA, or state-level agricultural regulations could impact product approvals, marketing claims, or distribution channels in key markets.
Market Adoption
Farmer adoption of new soil amendments can be slow due to risk aversion and established purchasing habits. Revenue projections depend on achieving significant market penetration.
Competition & IP
Larger agricultural companies with greater resources may develop competing products. Patent protection may be challenged or may not prevent all forms of competitive entry.
